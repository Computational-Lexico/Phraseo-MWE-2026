Submission Guidelines

All submissions must be made via the conference management system no later than March 8, 2026.

Submission link:
https://phraseo-mwe2026.sciencesconf.org/submission/submit?lang=en

Authors are invited to submit an extended abstract of 2–3 pages (PDF), including references. Submissions should clearly present the research question, methodology, data, and expected contributions related to phraseology and multi-word expressions.

Accepted abstracts will be included in the EURALEX 2026 Book of Abstracts. At least one author of each accepted contribution must register for the EURALEX congress and attend the workshop.

Submissions will be peer-reviewed by the Scientific Committee based on relevance, methodological soundness, and originality.
